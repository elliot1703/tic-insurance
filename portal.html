01

Why TIC

25 years in business. AFSL licensed. DAIA endorsed. Steadfast member. Clients like Maleny Dairies and Baxters. You have a serious operation — but your website is an Elementor template from 2021 with stock photos and generic copy. The gap between what TIC is and what ticib.com.au shows is enormous. 02

Why dark mode

Every insurance broker in Brisbane has a light, blue, corporate-looking site. Your clients don't work in corporate offices — they work on demolition sites, construction zones, and earthmoving pits. We designed in the visual language of the jobsite: charcoal background, safety amber accents, industrial typography. When a site manager sees this, they think "this broker gets my world." Nobody else in insurance has done this.

03

Why $3K, not $15K

This scope — market research, brand system, 3 creative directions, custom build, industry-specific copy — costs $8,000-$15,000 at a traditional agency and takes 4-6 weeks. We use AI to do the heavy research and production, then human review every decision. The quality is the same. The process is 10x faster. We pass the savings on because we'd rather have you as a long-term client than a one-time invoice.
